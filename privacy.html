Privacy Policy for Queens Rush

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Thank you for playing Queens Rush! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Collection and Use

We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information.

Queens Rush is a completely offline, local multiplayer and AI-based logic game. All game progress, settings, preferences (such as your chosen AI Bot name, theme, and haptics), and achievements are saved locally on your device using standard local storage mechanisms. None of this data ever leaves your device.

2. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs in the app. Because the app does not connect to the internet to function, there is no background data collection.

3. Children's Privacy

Our application is safe for children of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we add online features in the future that require data collection, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly and notify you through an app update.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at via the developer contact email listed on the Google Play Store page.
